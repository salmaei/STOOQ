About Us

Starting a new business is always difficult, it is especially difficult if you’re a student just starting out, which is why we created STOOQ! Our service allows you to manage, advertise, promote, and sell your products in our marketplace. Imagine you had a platform where businesses from different schools and universities could all come together and sell their products and services. Our aim is to introduce students into the market and help them connect with consumers and other businesses.

We follow a commission-based revenue model, where we’ll take a percentage of each item/service sold (yet to be set). We also offer a paid subscription service for businesses wanting to get more out of our platform.

Our Services

For Businesses

Free Version:

  • A platform to promote your products/services and reach customers.
  • Direct communication between businesses and customers through our chat service.

Paid Subscription:

  • A sales analytics page allowing businesses to keep track of products sold, revenue, trends and so on.
  • Access to features such as viewing how many consumers viewed your page and what products they were most interested in.
  • A verified tag.
  • More customization options for your business on our home screen to better highlight your products and services and make them stand out.
  • Priority access to our customer support service.

For Customers

  • Access to all businesses on our platform.
  • A filtering system that allows you to look through various categories and find specific products.
  • A secure E-payment service.
  • Direct communication with businesses.
